What is ArcLight

ArcLight is the first Neurorobotics company to develop functional and lifelike huamanoid robots that can adapt and evolve depending on its environment.

Mission

To provide a companion or a helper by creating humanoid robots that offer support and assistance to people.

Vision

We strive for a future where everyone can have easier and fulfilling lives.

Features

ArcLight androids can be trained for specific roles to meet there owners goals, but all ArcLight androids have the basic human attributes and abilities such as locomotion, communication, facial expression, artificial emotions, critical thinking, etc.


Made With Humanity in Mind

How it's Made: The Basics

1. Design

We plan and design the specific use case of the Android (e.g. healthcare, factory worker, police). What results is an android that excel in a specific role.

2. Hardware planning

We integrating only the necassary artificial human sensors (e.g. vision, but not pain receptors) and assemble the skeletal and artificial epidermis to better represent a human. This results in a near perfect resemblance of all observable human atributes.

3. AI components

We use our ArcLight neurodata technology to read human brain data and use that data to train an androids local neural network. The result is a perfect and efficient android that can do what humans can and cannot.

4. Testing

We use stress testing methods to identify failures in the machine. This ensures that the android works as expected.

Current ArcLight Models

ArcLight Model: Companion

Designed for everyone, built for daily tasks and interactions to keep you company. Has superior human capabilities in all areas (intelligence and strength).

Perfect for home roles such as a Nanny, Babysitter, etc.

Highly skilled in adapting to social environments and specialized in catering to owners emotional needs.

Arclight Model: Worker

This model is designed for business owners, employers and those who need manual assistance on projects.

Built for hard labour, extreme outdoor conditions and harsh chemical. Has superior human capabilities in all areas such as intelligence but also has exceptional physical abilities.

Perfect for labour intensive work or trades such as construction, oil rigs, plumber, etc. specializes in carrying objects as heavy as 500 kg.

ArcLight Model: Agent

This model is designed for high profile individuals, law enforcement agencies and intelligence agencies.

Built for daily tasks, harsh chemicals, and fast and precise movements. Has superior human capabilities in all areas but has exceptional intelligence and fast processing power.

Perfect for guarding or solving mentally heavy tasks such as bodyguarding, detective work, etc.

Exceptionally skilled at adapting to social and dangerous environments increasing the chance of both it and its owner's survival. Specializes in solving crime scenes as well as fulfilling duties no matter the situation .
